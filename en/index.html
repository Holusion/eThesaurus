eThesaurus

Medieval goldsmithing put to the test of digital modeling

e.thesaurus is a project in the field of art history and techniques, led by a team of French and Belgian academics, museum curators, and Holusion, a specialist in innovative digital display.

A project with two joint objectives in a scientific research framework

Development of 3D models of medieval goldsmithing pieces

with:

  • augmented reality restitution of artifacts
  • creation of a database (e.corpus) linked to 3D models
  • creation of innovative digital mediation devices

for:

  • promotion of a 3D catalog and analysis tool
  • valorization in a museum mediation context
